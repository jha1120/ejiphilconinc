THE COMPANY

EJI Philcon Incorporated is a Filipino owned company duly registered and existing under and by virtue of the laws of the Republic of the Philippines.

On September 2007, when an ordinary person with an extraordinary aspiration liked Engr. Emelito M. Balacuit, a Registered Mechanical Engineer by profession, established a sole proprietorship company, named JE & I Engineering Services which is engaged in installation, sale, repair and maintenance of air conditioning units. In less than a year, he widens his services engaging on design and installation of heating, ventilation and air conditioning (HVAC), ducting and piping works.

Now, with the fast growing economy and the advancement of technology entails the need to upgrade to be able to meet the demands and compete with some reputable and well recognized companies. Unfortunately, he found difficulties in doing things alone. His difficulties motivate him to shift gears. Through his enthusiasm and determination and positive attitude towards fulfilling a set of goals, he was able to invite and enlist people who will help him carry out his goals.

Henceforth, on February 02, 2015, a company named E.J.I. PHILCON INC. was ascertaining from sole proprietorship, JE & I Engineering and Services, with its incorporators came from diverse discipline. From side to side collaborative effort, the company stretched out its services through design and build, construct, implement and installed general engineering related development.

Slots in project planning such as: conceptualization, feasibility study, plans and tender documents preparation, cost and estimates, project supervision and management.
